Known for its flavorful Chinese and Asian dishes, Corner 17 is a go-to spot for students craving something different. 3. Mission Taco Joint – Delmar Loop

For those who love a good taco, Mission Taco Joint is a standout. It represents some of the Best food spots close to WashU campus that combine flavor with affordability.

4. Salt + Smoke

Salt + Smoke offers delicious smoked meats and comfort sides in a laid-back setting. Its hearty plates are ideal for those who want robust flavors without overspending.

5. Fitz’s Restaurant

A classic American diner with a twist, Fitz’s is famed for its burgers, hand-spun sodas, and nostalgic décor. This spot is one of the prime Places to Eat Near Washington University that promises both quality and a memorable experience.

6. Pi Pizzeria

If you’re in the mood for pizza, Pi Pizzeria is hard to beat. Known for its deep-dish style pies and creative toppings, this pizzeria offers fast, friendly service and wallet-friendly deals. It’s an excellent option among the Restaurants near Washington University for a quick slice between classes.

7. Three Kings Pub House

For a relaxed evening meal, Three Kings Pub House serves up comforting American fare. Its cozy atmosphere and affordable menu items make it a perfect spot for student get-togethers. This joint is a favorite among the Popular eateries near Washington University.

8. Pastaria

Pastaria brings authentic Italian flavors to campus with a focus on fresh pasta and creative pizza. This gem stands out as one of the Places to Eat Near Washington University that combines elegance with affordability.

9. Tony’s

For those who appreciate classic Italian dishes in a relaxed setting, Tony’s offers delicious risottos and pastas in generous portions and student-friendly prices. Tony’s is another excellent example of the Best food spots close to WashU campus that keep quality high while costs low.

As a casual eatery, Fox and Hounds offers some American staples.
